Escape and Evade - 60 Hours Commensing 01/05/2017

It was in the name of four children's charities - Mind, Anthony Nolan, Birmingham Children's Hospice, and Action Aid.

Our team reached Amsterdam by the end of our 60 hours. Our route went via Dover, Dunkrik, and Belgium.
